ABOUT MY WORK

I enjoy filming and using cameras, I make films about skateboarding or philosophies I work on.
A lot of my work have an aggresive approach towards the topic and have deeper meanings engraved in them.
I enjoy doing web development and messing around in VMs a lot.
I also do GFX & music as side projects.
My full-fledged films are idealogy based, and I try and relate every frame to the moral I wish to portray.
